# Daily Horoscope: Astrological Methodology Brief

**Audience:** Astrology research team
**Purpose:** Review our astrological methodology and identify improvements based on industry best practices
**Date:** November 3, 2025

---

## Overview

Our daily horoscope system generates personalized readings by combining:
1. **Natal-transit aspect analysis** (Swiss Ephemeris precision)
2. **Quantified astrological metrics** (23 specialized "astrometers")
3. **Natural language interpretation** (LLM)
4. **User personalization** (birth chart + behavioral patterns)

**Core Astrological Philosophy:**
- Transits as the primary timing mechanism
- Natal chart as the filter/context for transit interpretation
- Aspects as the language of planetary dialogue
- Houses providing life domain context

## Output Structure: The 7 Components

### 1. Daily Theme Headline
**Purpose:** One-sentence encapsulation of the day's dominant energy
**Length:** Max 15 words

**Astrological Sources:**
- The single strongest life domain meter (highest activity score)
- Quality of that domain (harmonious, challenging, or mixed based on aspect types)
- Overall astrological activity level

**Example:**
> "Jordan, career pressure is high today with challenging energy pushing you to prove yourself"

**Method:**
- Identify which life domain has most active natal-transit aspects
- Assess whether those aspects are harmonious (trines, sextiles) or challenging (squares, oppositions)
- State the concrete implication for that domain

**Questions for Review:**
- Is focusing on the single strongest domain appropriate, or should we integrate multiple themes?
- How do you typically prioritize when multiple areas are equally active?

---

### 2. Daily Overview
**Purpose:** Opening narrative explaining how the day feels AND why astronomically
**Length:** 3-4 sentences, 60-80 words

**Structure:**
1. **Emotional/energetic tone** - How the day feels overall
2. **Astronomical justification** - Which specific transits are causing this experience
3. **Sun sign connection** - How this relates to their core personality

**Astrological Sources:**
- Top 2-3 most active life domains (by aspect count and tightness)
- Exact transit positions (planet, degree, sign)
- Natal positions being aspected (planet, degree, sign)
- Aspect types and houses involved
- Sun sign archetypal traits

**Example:**
> "Jordan, today feels moderate but focused—like several things need your attention at once. Your career area is at high stakes **because** Pluto at 301° Aquarius just hit the exact square to your natal Saturn at 212° Scorpio, which is why authority and structure feel so tested right now. Your partnerships have friction **because** Venus at 185° Libra opposes your natal Mars at 5° Aries—that's the independence versus connection tension. As a Gemini, you usually juggle well, but today asks you to choose priorities rather than multitask."

**Questions for Review:**
- Do you typically explain the astronomical "why" to clients, or focus more on interpretation?
- Is the causal linkage (transit X → experience Y) how you conceptualize transits?
- Should we weight slower-moving transits (outer planets) more heavily than fast transits (Moon, Mercury)?

**Astrological Sources:**

### 4. Actionable Advice (3-Part Structure)

**DO:** Specific action with timing
**DON'T:** Shadow/pitfall to avoid
**REFLECT ON:** Journaling question addressing core planetary tension

**Astrological Sources:**
- Primary aspect's planetary archetypes (e.g., Pluto-Saturn = transformation vs. structure)
- Quality of aspect (challenging aspects → what to avoid; harmonious aspects → how to not waste opportunity)
- House themes providing context for action

**Example (Pluto square Saturn in 10th house):**
- **DO:** "Before 3 PM, write down exactly 3 career fears you're avoiding. Then write one small action you can take on each this week."
- **DON'T:** "Don't let Pluto's intensity make you burn bridges or quit impulsively—this transformation needs Saturn's patience, even though it feels urgent."
- **REFLECT ON:** "What would your career look like if you stopped performing and started being authentic?"

**Questions for Review:**
- Is the DO/DON'T/REFLECT structure useful, or would you recommend a different format?
- How do you typically translate planetary symbolism into concrete actions?
- Should timing be more specific (lunar hours, planetary hours) or is general timing sufficient?

## The Astrometer System: Quantifying Life Domains

### Core Concept

Instead of relying solely on qualitative interpretation, we quantify 23 specific life domains using two metrics:

1. **Intensity (0-100):** How MUCH astrological activity in this domain
2. **Harmony (0-100):** How WELL things are going (harmonious vs. challenging aspects)

### Calculation Method

For each meter, we:
1. Define which planets, houses, signs, and aspect types are relevant
2. Find all natal-transit aspects matching those criteria
3. Weight each aspect by:
   - Proximity to exact (tighter = stronger)
   - Planet importance (outer planets weighted higher)
   - Aspect type quality (trine = harmonious, square = challenging)
4. Aggregate into intensity and harmony scores

**Example: Career Ambition Meter**
- Relevant: Saturn, 10th house, Capricorn, MC
- Weights:
  - Transit Saturn aspects: High weight
  - 10th house transits: Medium-high weight
  - Capricorn transits: Medium weight
- Today's aspects:
  - Transit Pluto 301° square Natal Saturn 212° (10th house) → High intensity, Low harmony
  - Transit Jupiter 95° trine MC 93° → Medium intensity, High harmony
- Result: Intensity 82/100, Harmony 45/100 → "High Stakes" state

### Calibration: Empirical Backtesting

We've analyzed 2,500+ diverse birth charts to establish percentile-based norms:
- 50th percentile = 50/100 score (typical day)
- 75th percentile = 75/100 score (notably active)
- 90th percentile = 90/100 score (very intense)

**Daily Change Thresholds** (based on 855,000 daily transitions):
- **Stable** (50% of days): < 2 point change
- **Slow** (25% of days): 2-6 point change
- **Moderate** (15% of days): 6-11 point change
- **Rapid** (10% of days): > 11 point change

## House System Considerations

### Natal Charts
**Current:** Placidus system (geodetic latitude-dependent cusps)
**Rationale:** Most common in modern Western astrology

**Alternative Systems Supported:**
- Whole Sign (ancient, simplicity)
- Koch (similar to Placidus)
- Equal House (Ascendant-based)
- Campanus, Regiomontanus, etc.

### Solar Charts (V1 - No Birth Time)
**Current:** Whole Sign houses using Sun as Ascendant
**Method:**
- Sun sign = 1st house
- Next sign = 2nd house, etc.
**Rationale:** Consistent, predictable, works without birth time

**Questions for Review:**
- Is Placidus still industry standard, or has Whole Sign gained dominance?
- For daily transits, do you use natal houses or solar houses?
- How do you handle clients without birth time data?
- Should we offer house system selection (user preference)?

## Aspect Philosophy

### Current Orbs
- Major aspects: 5-7° (tighter for faster planets)
- Minor aspects: Disabled (quincunx, semi-square, etc.)

### Aspect Interpretation Priority
1. **Exact aspects** (< 1° orb) - Peak influence
2. **Applying aspects** (approaching exact) - Building energy
3. **Separating aspects** (past exact) - Waning energy
4. **Wide aspects** (> 3° orb) - Background influence

**Questions for Review:**
- Should we distinguish between applying and separating in daily readings?
- Do you use tighter orbs for transits vs. natal aspects?
- Should minor aspects be included for outer planet transits?
- How do you weight aspect importance (tight square vs. wide trine)?
- Should we include parallel/contraparallel aspects (declination)?

1. **New Moon** (0-45°) - New beginnings, intention-setting
